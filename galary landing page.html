About Us

Amia Islamic Charity was Founded in 2019 by visionary elders from Siipi Main Mosque in Bulambuli, Eastern Uganda, Amia Islamic Charity Foundation began with a mission to address the dire needs of orphans, widows, and vulnerable families.

Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda is a non-profit organization committed to alleviating poverty, providing educational resources, healthcare, and empowering communities through various charity-driven projects.

Our mission is To empower vulnerable communities in Eastern Uganda through sustainable support, education, and Islamic values, fostering self-reliance and dignity.

Our Vision is becoming a compassionate society where every individual, regardless of faith, has access to basic needs, education, and economic opportunities, inspired by Islamic principles of justice, equality, and mercy.
